Wheelchair Taxi

About us

We are a Taxi company located in Tel Aviv but are able to work around Israel. If you want to go to the airport, shopping, have a doctor's appointment or just want to get from one place to another without having to struggle getting in and out the car, we have the Taxi for you.

Our Taxis are designed to accomodate the most comfortable journey for disabled people. Without having to move, the Taxi can fit one wheelchair and more passengers.
